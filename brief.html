Revenue Model

Every trade executed through Sentinel generates a builder fee — a small percentage routed to our wallet on every Hyperliquid order. Users trade, we earn. No subscriptions needed at the trading layer.

The SaaS layer (web terminal, API access) will add tiered subscriptions on top — Free, Pro, and Enterprise.
